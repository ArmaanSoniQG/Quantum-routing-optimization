The problem

CBSA enforcement is rising. Mid-tier operators feel it most.

A hazmat placed wrong. A weight overflow on a tri-axle. A manifest mismatch nobody caught. By the time the inspector finds it, it's already cost you.

$1K+
Minimum CBSA fine when a load gets flagged. Documented at a mid-tier LCL forwarder in Delta, BC.
$160/day
Detention cost per container while held for inspection. Stacks every day the shipment is frozen.
Months
To rebuild reputation with a flagging officer. The fine is the small part — trust erosion is what compounds.
The solution

A digital check before the truck leaves the yard.

Shipment data in. A verified load plan out. Inspectors can see it, dispatchers can show it, drivers can follow it. The check runs before the truck rolls — not after the inspector finds the problem.

01

Send the manifest

Shipment data, equipment type, route, jurisdiction. Through your TMS, a CSV, or our intake form.

Pass or fail verdict with a load plan inspectors trust. Export it as an evidence document for the roadside.
